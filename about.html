About Solar Assist

Our Story

Solar Assist was founded with a clear vision: to provide unbiased, expert guidance for residential solar projects without the conflicts of interest that come with installation services. Based in Changanacherry, we serve clients throughout Kerala who are looking to harness the power of solar energy.
